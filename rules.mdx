The objective of Tic Tac Toe is to place three of your markers in a line before your opponent does. Every decision you make — which cell to claim, which threats to block — is aimed at completing a row, column, or diagonal before your opponent can do the same.

## The board

The game is played on a 3×3 grid of nine equal cells. Two players participate: one plays as **X** and the other as **O**. Player X always moves first. Each cell can hold exactly one marker, and once placed a marker cannot be moved or removed.

## Valid moves

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Place on an empty cell" icon="square">
    You may only place your marker on a cell that does not already contain a symbol. Every turn you must choose one empty cell.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No moves after the game ends" icon="ban">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Occupied cells are rejected" icon="circle-exclamation">

  </Card>

## Win conditions

A player wins the moment three of their markers occupy any of the following eight combinations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Row 1 — top horizontal" icon="grip-lines">
    All three cells in the top row contain the same marker: top-left, top-center, and top-right.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Row 2 — middle horizontal" icon="grip-lines">
    All three cells in the middle row contain the same marker: middle-left, middle-center, and middle-right.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Row 3 — bottom horizontal" icon="grip-lines">
    All three cells in the bottom row contain the same marker: bottom-left, bottom-center, and bottom-right.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Column 1 — left vertical" icon="grip-lines-vertical">
    All three cells in the left column contain the same marker: top-left, middle-left, and bottom-left.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Column 2 — center vertical" icon="grip-lines-vertical">
    All three cells in the center column contain the same marker: top-center, middle-center, and bottom-center.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Column 3 — right vertical" icon="grip-lines-vertical">
    All three cells in the right column contain the same marker: top-right, middle-right, and bottom-right.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Diagonal — top-left to bottom-right" icon="arrow-down-right">
    The three cells running from the top-left corner through the center to the bottom-right corner all contain the same marker.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Diagonal — top-right to bottom-left" icon="arrow-down-left">
    The three cells running from the top-right corner through the center to the bottom-left corner all contain the same marker.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  All eight winning combinations are checked automatically after every move. The game calls `revisarFila()` for rows, `revisarColumna()` for columns, `revisarDiagonalIzquierda()` for the top-left to bottom-right diagonal, and `revisarDiagonalDerecha()` for the top-right to bottom-left diagonal. You do not need to declare a win — it is detected and the board is locked immediately.
</Note>

## Draw rule

If all nine cells are filled and none of the eight winning combinations has been completed, the game is declared a draw. The `empate()` function confirms this by scanning every cell for an empty value — if none are found and no winner was detected, `juegoActivo` is set to `false`. No further moves are accepted. Click **Reiniciar juego** to start a new game.
